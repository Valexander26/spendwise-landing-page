Features

Everything your bank
should have built.

Item-level visibility into every purchase. Something no bank has ever offered.

📸

AI Receipt Scanning

Point your camera at any receipt. Every item read automatically in under 30 seconds. No typing, no manual entry — ever.

⏰

Return Deadline Tracker

Every receipt creates a return deadline automatically. Alerts at 7, 3 and 1 day before it expires. Never lose money again.

💬

Spending Memory

Ask anything in plain English. "How much on coffee last month?" Instant answers from your real purchase history.

📍

Receipt Radar

Spend 3 minutes in a shop and SpendWise knows. When you leave it reminds you to scan your receipt while it's still in your hand.

📊

Smart Insights

See exactly where your money goes — by category, by store, week on week and month on month. Real patterns from real purchases.

Household Plans

Share SpendWise across up to 5 family members. See combined household spending or individual breakdowns.

Three steps. That's it.

1

2

See every item

Every product, price and category appears automatically. Your history builds itself.

3

Ask anything

Type any question about your spending. Get an instant answer. Like a financial assistant in your pocket.
